Our Products

Our products are handled under controlled post-harvest processes and prepared in accordance with common export standards, making them suitable for wholesalers, distributors, and food industries worldwide.


1. Fresh Potatoes

Product Name : Fresh Potatoes (Export Grade)

Product Description

Our export-grade fresh potatoes are grown in Indonesia’s fertile highland regions, known for producing potatoes with firm texture, uniform shape, and stable shelf life. Each batch is carefully sorted and packed to meet international buyer requirements.

Technical Specifications

• Varieties : Granola

• Origin : Dieng Plateau, Indonesia

• Size Graded :

A. (Big) 3–4 pcs/kg
B. (Medium) 7–8 pcs/kg
C. (Small) 11–12 pcs/kg mm

(custom grading available)

• Skin Color : Light brown to yellow

• Flesh : Firm, low moisture loss

• Defects : Max. 3%

• Moisture : Natural, well-cured

• Shelf Life : 2–3 months (cold storage)

Application

• Fresh retail and wholesale markets

• Food service and hospitality

• Processing industries (fries, chips)

Packaging & Loading

• PP or mesh bags : 5 kg, 10 kg, 25 kg

• Carton boxes upon request

• Container loading : 20 FT / 40 FT reefer

2. Fresh Carrots

Product Name : Fresh Carrots (Export Grade)

Product Description

Harvested at optimal maturity, our export-grade carrots feature vibrant color, crisp texture, and consistent sizing. Strict cleaning and sorting processes ensure premium quality suitable for overseas shipment.

Technical Specifications

• Length : 15–22 cm

• Diameter : 2–4 cm

• Color : Bright orange

• Moisture Content : Natural

• Defects : Max. 3%

• Shelf Life : 4–6 weeks (cold storage)

Applications

• Fresh market and supermarket chains

• Food processing and juice industries

Packaging & Loading

• Plastic bags or cartons : 5 kg, 10 kg, 20 kg

• Palletized export packing available

• Reefer container shipment

3. Sweet Potatoes

Product Name : Sweet Potatoes (Export Grade)

Product Description

Our sweet potatoes are sourced from selected farms and processed with care to preserve freshness, natural sweetness, and texture. Suitable for fresh consumption and industrial use.

Technical Specifications

• Size : 150–400 g per piece

• Skin : Smooth, clean

• Flesh Color : White / Orange / Purple (subject to availability)

• Moisture : Controlled

• Defects : Max. 5%

• Shelf Life : 2–3 months (ventilated storage)

Applications

• Fresh consumption

• Food processing and snack industries

Packaging & Loading

• Woven bags or cartons 10 kg, 20 kg

• Export-standard labeling

• Container shipment

4. Whole Mature Coconut

Product Name : Whole Mature Coconut (Export Grade)

Product Description

Our whole peeled mature coconuts are carefully selected, fully husked, and neatly trimmed to meet export market requirements. Known for high oil content and long shelf life.

Technical Specifications

• Weight : 0.8–1.2 kg per piece

• Maturity : Fully mature (12 months)

• Husk : Fully removed

• Moisture Loss : Minimal

• Shelf Life : Up to 60 days (proper storage)

Applications

• Coconut milk and oil processing

• Food and beverage industries

• Wholesale and export distribution

Packaging & Loading

• Mesh bags : 10–15 pcs per bag

• Palletized export packing available

5. Palm Broomstick

Product Name : Palm Broomstick (Export Quality)

Product Description

Our palm broomsticks are produced from selected palm midribs, naturally sun-dried and carefully sorted to ensure strength, flexibility, and uniformity for export markets.

Technical Specifications

• Length : 90–120 cm

• Diameter : 3–5 mm

• Moisture Content : < 15%

• Color : Natural yellowish

• Condition : Clean, mold-free

Applications

• Traditional and industrial broom manufacturing

• Household and commercial cleaning tools

Packaging & Loading

• Bundled or compressed bales

• Suitable for 20 FT / 40 FT containers
